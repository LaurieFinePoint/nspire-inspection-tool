📱 iPhone or iPad? Read This First — One-Time Setup

This tool works 100% offline — you do not need internet or cell service during inspections. But you must save the file to your device before you go to a property. Do this once, while you have wifi:

  1. Open this link in Safari (not Chrome, not another browser — Safari only).
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen. It looks like a box with an arrow pointing up.
  3. Scroll down in the menu that appears and tap "Save to Files."
  4. Tap "On My iPhone" or "On My iPad." Do NOT choose iCloud Drive.
  5. Tap Save in the top right corner.

How to open the tool at a property (no internet needed):

  1. Tap the Files app on your iPhone or iPad. It looks like a blue folder.
  2. Tap "Browse" at the bottom.
  3. Tap "On My iPhone" or "On My iPad."
  4. Find the file named NSPIRE_Field_Inspection_Tool_v8.html and tap it.
  5. It will open in Safari and you can begin your inspection.

⚠️ Do NOT open this tool by tapping a link in an email or text message when you are at a property — that requires internet. Always use the Files app as described above.
